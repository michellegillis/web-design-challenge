Unlike humidity, there are clear patterns available for analysis in this scatter plot. We can see that cloudiness lines up at certain points and tends to cluster based on latitude. Latitudes between -40 and -20 show distinct gathering of data points at 0% cloudiness. 0 through 20 latitude show patterns at 0%, ~75%, and ~90% cloudiness. 20 to 40 latitudes tend to gather at approximately the same points as 0 to 20. Latitudes of 40 and above show much higher % cloudiness, and have high numbers of cities at ~75% and 90%.
