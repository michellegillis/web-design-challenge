This comparison between latitude and wind speed shows some general patterns across the board. Most wind speeds trend below 15 mph regardless of latitude, however the highest wind speeds occur only at latitudes above 40. This may be in part due to the lack of data available on cities at the equivalent distance south of the equator beyond -40 latitude. Wind speeds also seem to drop dramatically with close proximity to the equator, with latitudes of exactly 0 having wind speeds of no more than 10mph for the given date.
